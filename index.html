Alky HF Acid Testing

  • 1. You'll weigh the caught sample HF cylinder and the empty crucible cup.
  • 2. Start the heaters to an LED setting of 130 degrees C and an LED setting of 305 degrees C. This should give a sample temperature of 126 degrees F and 250 degrees F
  • Cook at 126 degrees F for 15 minutes, then at 250 degrees F for 5 minutes. Weigh crucible after it cools to room temperature.
  • 3. Click ASO START button to begin.

  • 1. To a 250ml Erlenmeyer flask add 2 mL of 1.0 NaOH and Dilute to 50mL with D.I. water. Add several drops of phenolphthalein indicator. Connect to apparatus.
  • 2. To a 500ml Erlenmeyer flask add 48mL of 1.0 NaOH and dilute to 150mL with D.I. water. Add a magnetic stir bar and several drops of phenolphthalein indicator. Connect to apparatus.
  • 3. Attach HF bomb to scrubber system. Slowly add acid until phenolphthalein color fades, then for 3-4 minutes longer. Close sample valve.
  • 4. Click HF PURITY START button to begin.
